Mitchell Street Phillbillies

The Mitchell Street Phillbillies are a seven piece cover band based in Roxborough. They dip their toes in many different genres, and exhibit a unique sound that features the trumpet, the saxophone, and the mandolin, in addition to your regular cover band fare like guitars and drums. They've been playing local shows in Northwest Philadelphia for more than three years, and always give the people a good time. Come check them out next time they play, you won't be disappointed!
